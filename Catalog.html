Graduate programmes

As undergraduate seniors, students may apply for admission to the department's doctoral program. Contact COMPSSA Graduate Office with questions at grad-ap@compssa.ug.edu.

COMPSSA does not offer a terminal master's degree. A master's degree is earned once you are accepted and enrolled as a doctoral candidate in the COMPSSA Department.
