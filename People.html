WELCOME FROM THE PRESIDENT

"Welcome to Computer Science! Our Department is outfitted with some of the most talented and dedicated CS researchers and educators in the world, and every single one of them is eager to provide you with the scientific and computational skills needed to succeed in industry, research, and academia. All of our CS degree programs furnish you with the foundational knowledge required for all computational work, but after that, you have broad authority over what direction you'd like to pursue."
